# DISATEQ Vendor - Operational Rules

## Product Philosophy

DISATEQ Vendor is an operational commercial platform focused on:
- simplicity;
- operational continuity;
- speed;
- resilience;
- and real-world usability.

The product is designed for small businesses operating in real environments with limited connectivity and modest hardware.

---

## Core Product Principles

- Maintain offline-first philosophy.
- Preserve operational continuity at all times.
- Prioritize operational UX over visual complexity.
- Avoid transforming the system into a complex ERP.
- Favor fast workflows and low cognitive load.
- Maintain install → open → sell simplicity.
- Synchronization must remain silent and non-intrusive.
- Hardware limitations are part of the design reality.

---

## Architecture Rules

- Respect the existing project structure before proposing reorganizations.
- Avoid unnecessary abstractions or enterprise-style architecture inflation.
- Prefer incremental consolidation over aggressive refactors.
- Do not create duplicate structures or parallel implementations.
- Shared logic must remain simple and understandable.
- Scalability must not break operational simplicity.

---

## UI / UX Rules

- Prioritize operational density and clarity.
- Avoid excessive whitespace and oversized components.
- Avoid generic SaaS dashboard aesthetics.
- The UI should feel modern, clean, and operational.
- Visual hierarchy should help speed and continuity of operation.
- Animations and effects must remain subtle and purposeful.

---

## Workflow Rules

- Validate builds and runtime behavior frequently.
- Prefer small validated steps over large speculative changes.
- Do not modify unrelated modules without justification.
- Preserve continuity and stability before introducing new patterns.

---

## AI Collaboration Rules

- AI must assist implementation, not redefine the product vision.
- Do not invent architecture unrelated to current operational needs.
- Do not restructure the project without explicit approval.
- Avoid speculative systems, placeholder abstractions, or unnecessary frameworks.
- Respect DISATEQ operational philosophy at all times.

---

## Source of Truth

- The repository is the operational source of truth.
- Git history protects continuity.
- Operational stability has priority over theoretical elegance.
- Human direction overrides autonomous AI decisions.
